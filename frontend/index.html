About Health Record Kerala

Building Kerala's digital health infrastructure for a healthier tomorrow.

Our Mission

Health Record Kerala is a flagship e-governance initiative by the Government of Kerala aimed at creating a centralized, secure, and accessible digital health record system for all 3.5 crore residents of the state.

We envision a Kerala where every citizen has instant access to their complete medical history, eliminating redundant tests, reducing healthcare costs, and enabling better-informed medical decisions.

Technology & Security

Our platform is built on enterprise-grade infrastructure with AES-256 encryption, multi-factor authentication, and ISO 27001 certified data centers located within India.

All data is stored in compliance with India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023, ensuring complete regulatory compliance and citizen data sovereignty.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information you provide during registration, including name, mobile number, date of birth, district, and blood group. We also collect health records you upload. No data is collected without your explicit consent.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely to provide the Health Record Kerala service — to generate your unique Health ID, store records securely, and enable authorized healthcare providers to access them when you consent via QR code. We never sell your data.

3. Data Security

All personal and health data is encrypted using AES-256 encryption at rest and TLS 1.3 in transit. Our servers are in India and comply with ISO 27001 security standards. We conduct regular security audits.

4. Your Rights

Under India's DPDP Act 2023, you may access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time via your dashboard or by contacting us. We respond to all requests within 30 days.

5. Data Retention

Records are retained while your account is active. Upon deletion, all data is removed within 90 days unless legally required to retain.

6. Contact

Privacy queries: dpo@healthrecord.kerala.gov.in or call 1800-599-0019.
